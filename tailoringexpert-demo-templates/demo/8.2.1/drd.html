Document Requirement Definitions (DRDs)
Scope The contract holder shall establish and supply documents as specified in DEMO document "Product Assurance & Safety Requirements for DEMO Projects“.
These documents are required to support hardware quality and reliability, to provide visibility for evaluating quality status of hardware and software, and to provide visibility for assessing the Product Assurance Programme.
Purpose A model set of Document Requirements Description (DRD) sheets is provided below and shall provide more detailed information of required data by DEMO and shall also provide example in case of applicable form sheets.In case of existing appropriate in-house form sheets the contractor shall use his own forms in any case but the form sheets must reflect all required data of this specification respectively in accordance to the DRD specification annexed below.
